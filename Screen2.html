June 17, 1972

Frank Willis

You arrive at the Watergate Complex, and interview Frank Willis, a security guard at the Complex. He informs you that he noticed tape covering the latches of doors in the complex, while doing a security check. He states he removed tape from the doors. He also states he thought nothing of it. When he returned an hour later, he noticed that someone had re-taped the locks, that's when he called the police. The police inform you that when they arrived they noticed five

men inside the Democraticc National Committee (DNC) headquarters, inside the complex. They arrested the five men, and charged them with attempted burglary, and attempted interception of telephone and other communications. The five

men were identified as Virgilio Gonzalez, Bernard Barker, James W. McCord, Jr., Eugenio Martinez, and Frank Sturgis.
